12-Week Webinar Series Objective
The six pillars of a creative business.

You'll identify which structural gap is costing you the most money — and the first action required to close it. For African creatives who are 2–5 years in, still earning inconsistently — and know it's time to build real structure.

01 02 03 04 05 06
01
Intellectual Property
Your talent has commercial value beyond service. Learn to own, protect and monetise what you create.
02
Pricing
Move from reactive quoting to intentional pricing that reflects your full value and business costs.
03
Branding
Build a brand identity that attracts the right clients and commands premium positioning in your market.
04
AI/Digital Leverage
Use digital tools and platforms to multiply your reach and revenue without multiplying your time.
05
Market Access
Identify and enter the right markets — local and global — without connections or relocation.
06
Distribution Systems
Build channels that deliver your work to buyers consistently — so income doesn't stop when you stop selling.
